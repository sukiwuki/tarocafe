Mahlyf Mahrulez

The enigmatic owner of TARO CAFE

Mahlyf Mahrulez started Taro Cafe with a vision. That vision, precisely, was to make a lot of cash. So, Mahlyf decided to make her first drink the thing that would help her do just that. Mahlyf spent years trying to perfect the ideal Taro Milk Tea that would wow everyone around her. And when she did, Taro Cafe was born.

The grand vision of Mahlyf Mahrulez came true-- and it happened quite fast. The whole nation came to love her signature drink so much that she had enough money to travel to all the taro fields on Earth. Mahlyf now resides on Mars with her doggy Vib. The last thing we ever heard her say was "It's my life, my rules."
